Experience Kayaking!

April 03, 2024
by: Armin Nafisa

Enjoy Kayaking Experiences with families.

Visitors can enjoy a variety of activities, such as snorkeling and scuba diving to explore the coral reefs, boat rides around the island, and stargazing on clear nights. Other attractions include walking along the shoreline, collecting shells, and cycling through the island's scenic paths. Its peaceful atmosphere, combined with its unique food and activities, makes Saint Martin's Island a favorite destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

The island is famous for its fresh and flavorful seafood, including fish, lobsters, crabs, and squid, often served grilled or fried with local spices. Coconut water, a refreshing treat, is a must-try, as the island is abundant in coconut trees.
